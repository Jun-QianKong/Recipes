Transforming Furikake Gohan

A delicious chicken don glazed with a luscious golden, egg coating

An original take on the classical Furikake Gohan, made of dried ingredients like seaweed, sesame seeds, dried fish and many more.

Ingredients

  • 7 Chicken Wings
  • 4 eggs
  • pinch of salt
  • Diced spring onion
  • 1 tbs Sesame oil, Grated ginger, sake, mirin, sugar
  • 700cc Bonito broth
  • 50cc light soy sauce

Recipe Instructions

  1. Heat sesame oil in frying pan and saut√© chicken wings until golden brown on both sides
  2. Put chicken wings, sesame oil, grated ginger, bonito broth, sake, mirin, sugar, light soy sauce into a pot and turn on high until it boils. Skim scum off top, reduce heat to low, and simmer until broth level is reduced by half
  3. Pour broth into a container to cool. Once cooled, place in refrigerator to chill and harden. Debone chicken wings and cut into strips.
  4. Make the egg curds. Crack eggs into bowl, add sugar and a pinch of salt, and whisk. Pour into frying pan on low heat, stirring rapidly to create the curds. Once done, move to plate.
  5. Once broth cardens, remove from container and cut aspic into 1cm x 1cm squares.
  6. Pour aspic, egg curds, and chicken strips over hot rice. Sprinkle with diced spring onion and enjoy!
